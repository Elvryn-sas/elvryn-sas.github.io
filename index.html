Core Capabilities

Everything your
school needs
in one place

From daily attendance to monthly reports, Elvryn handles the entire workflow so teachers can focus on what matters — teaching.

01
One-Tap Marking

Mark an entire class as present, absent, or late in seconds. Designed for the pace of a real classroom — no wasted time.

02
Student Data Security

Student records are encrypted and stored securely. Role-based access ensures only authorized staff see sensitive information.

03
Live Sync

Attendance marked on a teacher's phone appears instantly on the admin dashboard. No delays, no manual transfers.

04
Multi-Class Management

Handle multiple sections, grades, and subjects from a single interface. Switch between classes without losing context.

05
Automated Reports

Generate daily, weekly, or monthly attendance reports automatically. Identify patterns and flag students at risk of falling behind.

06
Works Offline

No internet in the classroom? Mark attendance offline and it syncs automatically the moment connectivity is restored.
